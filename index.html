How to Find Your Census Tract & Block Group

You can find your number using the official City of Long Beach map. This is the easiest method.

  1. Go to the Long Beach Census Map.
  2. In the search box on the left, type your full property address and press Enter.
  3. A list will appear below the search bar. Click on the row that says **"Census Block Group"**.
  4. The details will then be displayed, including the **"TRACT"** and **"BLKGRP"** numbers.
